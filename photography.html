Photo Gallery

Photography is a recent venture for me, A friend suggested that it would enlarge my spectrum and he was right! I try and take my camera wherever I can when visiting new places because there's always something that will catch your eye when you least expect it. To see more of my photography you can check my Flickr page here.

Castle Gòrach

Castle Gorach

Canon EOS 1220D, edited in Photoshop

Castle Gòrach is a ruined folly on Tulloch Hill, north of Dingwall in the Scottish Highlands. It was built by the Davidson family, the then estate owners of the area.

As you can see from my Art page, Castle Gorach is one of my favourite subjects as well as being curious, it is on my doorstep, and crops up in my work from time to time. This work was taken recently as the trees growing on top of the ruin were removed by vandals in the early 2000s.

Here I wanted to experiment a little with Photoshop and give a more artistic effect.

Rogie Falls

Rogie Falls

Canon EOS 1220D

Rogie Falls are located just off the A835 north of Contin. The river is the River Blackwater which is a tribuatary of the River Conon.

I took a long exposure here to get the silky water effect. Just to the right of the shot is a salmon ladder for salmon to traverse the falls.
