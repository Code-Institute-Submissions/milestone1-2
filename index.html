Welcome! Fàilte!

Original Art and Photographic works from the Scottish Highlands

Castle Gòrach, near Dingwall edited in Photoshop

Welcome to my site! Art has always played a part of my life and living in the Scottish Highlands has always had a key influence in my art and photography. I have been drawing and painting for the most part of my adult life with photography being a recent venture to supplement my artwork. I mostly deal with landscapes but I am continually expanding my subject material as time goes on and as new media and technology become available so watch This space!

Please feel free to browse my Art and Photography galleries to view my work, I can do commissions on request, please feel free to contact me via the Contact page detailing what you have in mind and we can touch base to see if I can fulfil your requirements. I work mostly in pastels but also deal with Oil, Acrylic and various other media.

Any queries please feel free to contact me via the Contact Page.
