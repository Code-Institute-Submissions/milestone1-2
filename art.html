Art Gallery

Art has played a part of most of my life, there's never a moment when I'm outdoors something catches my eye and I think how I can make something with it. The Highland landscape is one of my favourite subjects and features a lot in my work, but I'm always looking at new subjects and styles to experiment with so watch this space for any new developments!
